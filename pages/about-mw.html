About Me

Who I Am

My name is Sonny Torres and I am currently a commercial real estate analyst and programmer who lives in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex.

Why Data Science?

In 2015 I graduated from the University of North Texas with a Bachelors Degree in Finance. What attracted me to Finance was the abundance of complex data involved in the Stock Market and the analysis required to understand the data. My love for making sense of data lead me to work as an analyst at a small financial consulting company that specializes in securitized commercial real estate loans. In this role I have gained invaluable experience with manipulating data in many different ways. After pushing the limits of excel, I decided to commit to learning how to program in December 2016 and it has been a very rewarding (and challenging!) journey.

Going forward

My goal for this blog is to showcase what I have learned and document my ongoing journey as an aspiring Data Scientist. Feel free to contact me.
